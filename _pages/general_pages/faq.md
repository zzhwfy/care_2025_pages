## Data

**Q: Can I use external data or pre-trained models?**

A: Publicly available external data is allowed, provided it is publicly available before the test results submission deadline. Private annotations of such data are prohibited. Open pre-trained models are also allowed.

---

## Publication

**Q: Will there be a proceedings paper?**

A: We are considering submitting a proceeding. Paper selection is based on quality, with a focus on novel and effective methods that facilitate the translation of medical AI into real-world clinical practice. Links to proceedings of previous CARE events: [CARE 2024](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-3-031-87009-5), [CARE 2025](https://link.springer.com/book/9783032162700).

---

**Q: Will there be a benchmark paper?**

A: A benchmark paper may be submitted depending on the specific situation. Among our previous events, [MMWHS'17](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.media.2019.101537), [MS-CMRSeg'19](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.media.2022.102528), and [MyoPS'20](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.media.2023.102808) have published benchmark papers. CARE 2025 has also prepared a benchmark paper for the liver track.

---

## Awards

**Q: How are awards determined?**

A: The award policy is listed on the [homepage](/care_2026/). Best paper and runners-up are selected based on paper quality. Best performance awards are based on test results.
